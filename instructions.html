Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 1 can marinara sauce
  • 2 teaspoons basil
  • half a onion
  • 1 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • oil

Instructions

Steps

  1. Boil water and add salt before placing the pasta into the water. Boil the pasta for around 11 minutes. I prefer 11 minutes as the pasta is a bit more firm. If you prefer a softer pasta, boil for a bit longer.
  2. Drain the pasta after it is done and set aside.
  3. Dice up the onions and mushrooms.
  4. Heat a pan up on medium high heat. Add a bit of oil to the pan and wait until the pan is hot.
  5. Place the onions on the pan and fry until golden brown.
  6. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until done. Add seasonings to taste.
  7. Add the mushrooms in and let it cook on lowheat.
  8. Add the marinara sauce and the pasta.
